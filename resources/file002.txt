paris is a city of lights fashion and romance it is the capital of france and one of the most visited cities 
in the world the eiffel tower notre dame cathedral and louvre museum are popular tourist attractions 
paris is also known for its beautiful streets filled with cafes bakeries and shops selling clothes perfumes 
and books artists and writers have long found inspiration in the charming atmosphere of montmartre and 
the left bank the seine river runs through the city and you can enjoy boat rides that show you many 
historic landmarks the french cuisine is another reason why people visit paris croissants cheese wine 
and fine dining are essential parts of parisian life the metro system is extensive and connects all 
districts of the city paris is also home to many universities and research centers that attract students 
from different countries the city hosts international events and exhibitions that make it a global cultural hub
