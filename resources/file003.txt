moscow is the largest city in russia and its capital it is a political economic and cultural center with 
a population of over ten million people the kremlin and red square are iconic landmarks of moscow 
the city has a rich history going back many centuries it played a crucial role during world war two and 
was a major part of the soviet union era today moscow is a modern city with skyscrapers large shopping 
centers and vibrant nightlife the moscow metro is one of the busiest in the world and is known for its 
beautifully decorated stations the bolshoi theatre and the pushkin museum are popular among art lovers 
people in moscow enjoy long walks in parks and along the moskva river winter can be very cold with 
temperatures dropping far below zero snow covers the city and creates a magical atmosphere during 
the holiday season moscow continues to grow and attract business tourists and professionals from 
around the world
